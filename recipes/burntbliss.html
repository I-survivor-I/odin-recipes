BurntBliss

Golden baked fries paired with tender salmon, drizzled in rich olive oil for a satisfying, wholesome meal

The Ingredients:

  • frozen/unfrozen salmon fillets, with no skin
  • potatoes
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • olive oil

The steps:

    SALMON
  1. if the salmon is frozen wait until its unfrozen will take about 2-3 hours
  2. wash the salmon with cold water
  3. dry the salmon
  4. season the salmon with salt + black pepper
  5. add a layer of little bit of olive oil
    POTATOES

    BAKING
  1. pre-heat the oven for 250 degree for about 20-30 mins
  2. put a baking paper on the tray
  3. brush olive oil on the baking paper
  4. put the salmon and potatoes on the tray
  5. insert the goods, wait arround 15-18 mins, check it every now-and-then
