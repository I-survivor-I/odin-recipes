SizzleScramble

A simple, delicious dish of fluffy scrambled eggs with juicy tomatoes and sweet onions, perfect for any time of day

The Ingredients:

  • eggs
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • olive oil

The steps:

  1. dice the onion
  2. dice the tomatoes
  3. put the pan on the stove
  4. add a layer of little bit of olive oil
  5. insert the onion and cook for about 1-2 mins
  6. insert the tomatoes and coor for about 2-4 mins
  7. insert the eggs and cook until it has a satisfying look to it
